How It Works

The PH@TT program has been specially designed to support and balance your body’s natural functions, supporting your digestive system to heal and work efficiently therefore encouraging it to lose weight as it needs to.

We achieve this through resting and repairing the gut with meal plans designed by our nutritionist, we eat real food (no shakes), along with what we feel are the best natural nutrition supplements on the market. You may lose up to 15% of your body weight in just one (30 day) round and the program can be repeated if you have more weight to lose.

If your goal is simply gut health supporting improved health overall, we adjust the modified calorie diet plan to accommodate this.

The First 3 Days

This is where you get off the blood sugar roller coaster and begin to get your appetite under control, you gut begins to function more efficiently. During these three days, you will begin your natural supplement support, this is high quality "clean green and natural nutritional" supplements. With the right nutrition and supplement support you quickly become a fat burning body rather than a fat storage body. Typically, after the second day you will also begin to overcome food cravings.

Fat Loss and Detox

This is the weight loss stage as your body will start to release fat, reduce food cravings and stabilise. With food cravings gone and fat burning increased along with good nutrition you will likely feel greater mental clarity and enjoy the increased energy. This stage is vital to help improve digestion, support your gut to heal along with reducing inflammation in the body. This step will help lift a low metabolism, may improve the function of phase 1 & 2 of the liver and supports and improves bowel function.

On this stage, you eat a simple and light modified calorie intake, we mono eat, i.e. one protein and one vegetable at each meal. We know that the bigger range of foods you put on your plate, the harder your gut must work; therefore, we keep it simple to help the gut to rest and restore.

Therefore, the nutritional supplements are important, they help to support your gut to do its job well. This step typically lasts 27 days. However, there is the option to extend this phase.

Life

This step is all about teaching/supporting you to sustain a healthy realistic lifestyle. It is designed to keep you at your new healthy weight by continuing to support your gut to work efficiently and helping to keep your hormones in balance. You eat a more varied and higher calorie diet. You start to notice how changes in your diet make you feel.

All 3 stages are important for successful weight loss, and if you want to lose more weight you simply go back to step 1 and repeat!

General Tips To Get You Started

Water: One of the key factors along with many others that will determine your success on this program is consuming adequate levels of water. It is imperative that you sip 3 litres of water daily to not only assist with the detoxification process but to assist with suppressing your appetite, and the chemical breakdown of fat stores. Herbal Teas (not fruit teas) are acceptable.

Digestion: It is important to consume your dinner 2 hours prior to going to bed to allow adequate time for digestion. This will ensure you don’t weigh in heavier in the morning.

Exercise: If you are not one to exercise normally it is not recommended you start strenuous exercise when doing this modified calorie program. If you already have an exercise regime it is recommended you do not do any more than normal. Please do discuss individually with your mentor.

About Me
