Hi, I’m Steph from PH@TT (Putting Health At The Top), and I am here to support you through a 30 day program designed to improve your gut health and encourage weight loss.

Here at PH@TT we believe that through a healthy diet, with a focus on caring for your gut, you can achieve your health goals of weight loss (up to 15% of your body weight in just one round) and/or a healthy gut whilst still maintaining an overall sense of well-being. Improved gut health and diet is key to healthy weight, mood, sleep, energy and more. So much so that our program does not even encourage strenuous exercise in order for you to reach your goals.

I am passionate about health and can help you to achieve your health and fitness goals through ongoing mentorship in the form of support and education, and this is what sets us apart from other weight loss programs. As a mentor I am invested in your health and will work hard alongside you to improve it. If you have any inquires about this program, please feel free to get in contact with me. Whether you just have a couple of questions or feel ready to get started, I’d be happy to hear from you.
