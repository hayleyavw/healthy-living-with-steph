How It Works

The PH@TT program has been specially designed to balance your body’s natural functions therefore encouraging it to lose weight as it needs to. We do this through resting and repairing the gut with our meal plans designed by our nutritionist, along with what feel are the best natural nutrition supplements on the market. Results obviously vary between individuals, however you may lose up to 15% of your body weight in just one (30 day) round and the program can be repeated if you have more weight to lose.

The First 3 Days

This is where you get off the blood sugar roller coaster and get your appetite under control and you start to burn and release fat. During these three days, you will begin your natural nutritional supplement support, this is high quality "clean green and natural nutritional" supplements. With the right nutrition and supplement support you quickly become a fat burning body rather than a fat storage body. Typically, after the second day you will begin to overcome food cravings and your body will start to release fat.

Fat Loss and Detox

This is the weight loss stage as your body will start to release fat, reduce food cravings and stabilise. With food cravings gone and fat burning increased, you will feel greater mental clarity and enjoy the increased energy. This stage is vital to help improve digestion, heal your gut and reduce inflammation in the body. This step lifts a low metabolism, may improve the function of phase 1 & 2 of the liver and supports and improves bowel function.

On this stage you calorie count and mono eat, i.e. one protein and one vegetable at each meal. The bigger range of foods you put on your plate, the harder your gut has to work, therefore we cut back to help it heal instead. Life

This step is all about teaching you how to sustain a healthy lifestyle going forward. It is designed to keep you at your new healthy weight by keeping your hormones in balance. You no longer have to mono eat or calorie count, and you may even choose to stop taking supplements if you no longer feel you need them.

All 3 stages are important for successful weight loss, and if you want to lose more weight you simply go back to step 1 and repeat!

Who am I?

Hi, I’m Steph.

I am a Registered Nurse of 30 years and now also a Senior consultant with Putting Health at The Top, (PH@TT).

I have completed the PH@TT program myself and maintain my 16kg weight loss now comfortably. I believe caring for our gut is a significant key to caring for our health both physically and mentally. I would love to work with you to support you to make the healthy changes you would like to see in your life.

After many years working in varied areas of general health I spent 9 years working in the addiction sector and I have been supporting people to make healthy and sustainable changes for many years. I continue currently to also work in the field of mental health providing 1 on 1 support and in group settings. I utilise motivational interviewing techniques and CBT (cognitive behavioural therapy) strategies to support us to begin to change the way we think and react to food and life stressors. I incorporate simple mindfulness and sensory modulation modules of practice to support change.

I look forward to joining you on your journey to better health,
